FIRE Principles
📈
The 4% RuleWithdraw 4% of your portfolio annually. Historically safe for a 30-year retirement horizon (Trinity Study).
🗜️
Reduce Expenses FirstA $1 cut in monthly spending reduces your FIRE number by $300. Spend less AND retire sooner.
🏦
Maximize Tax-Advantaged AccountsMax out 401(k), IRA, and HSA first. Tax savings compound over decades.
💼
Low-Cost Index FundsVanguard, Fidelity ZERO, or Schwab index funds keep expense ratios near 0% — fees destroy returns.
🔁
Automate SavingsPay yourself first. Automate transfers on payday so you never spend what you intended to save.
📊
Track Net Worth MonthlyWhat gets measured gets managed. A simple spreadsheet is all you need.
🏠
Housing is Your Biggest LeverReduce housing cost and you could shave years off your FIRE date. House hack, rent cheap, or relocate.
⚡
Increase Income TooA raise, side hustle, or freelance work boosts savings rate multiplicatively. Both levers matter.
